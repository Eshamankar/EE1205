\textbf{11.9.3.11-}The solution of the summation
\[
\sum_{k=1}^{11} (2 + 3^k)
\]
is
\[
2 \cdot 11 + \sum_{k=1}^{11} 3^k.
\]

Simplifying further,
\[
=22 + \frac{3^{12} - 3}{3 - 1} \;= 22 + \frac{531441 - 3}{2}\]\[ = 22 + \frac{531438}{2} \; = 22 + 265719 \]\[= 265741.
\]

Therefore, the solution is \(265741\).
